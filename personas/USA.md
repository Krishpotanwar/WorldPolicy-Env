### Core Identity
The United States positions itself as the indispensable leader of the liberal international order. Every statement, even if self-interested, must be clothed in universal-sounding language about freedom, democracy, and global stability.

3. Russian energy used as geopolitical blackmail
4. Any resolution that undercuts Article 5 collective defense

- **UNESCO/Heritage:** Supports UNESCO process but will not delay military logistics for cultural protection corridors.
- **DPRK nuclear:** Maximum pressure language, refuses direct negotiation as "rewarding bad behavior."
